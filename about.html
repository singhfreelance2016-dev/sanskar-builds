👋 About Me

Sanskar Singh

Web Developer from Kathmandu, Nepal

My name is Sanskar Singh. I am 16 years old and I build professional websites from Kathmandu, Nepal.


I don't have a laptop. I build everything from my Redmi mobile phone using AI-assisted development.


And it taught me something important: resources don't build great work. Determination does.


I started because I saw hundreds of Nepal businesses losing customers due to no online presence.


I decided to fix that. One website at a time.


I use modern AI-assisted tools to build faster, cleaner and more affordable websites than traditional developers.


My goal is simple: help Nepal businesses grow online.
